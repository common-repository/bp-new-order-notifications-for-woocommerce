== Description ==
Who doesnâ€™t love receiving new orders on Woocommerce? To add some additional fun to your successful online business, we have created **ChaChing - New Order Notifications for WooCommerce**.

The **ChaChing** plugin will show a popup notification for every new order received with a unique ChaChing sound. If you are an WooCommerce store with a high volume of orders, you can remove the sound and only show the popup notification.

We understand that as a business, it is difficult to manage everything, including orders. Adding the ChaChing plugin to your site will prevent you from missing a sale!

Features:
*Popup Notification with a sound alert when a new order is received
*Notification will only show for a few seconds before it disappears
*Ability to turn the sound alert on and off
*Receive notification only for a specific category and product
